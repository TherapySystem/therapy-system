Mission

We aim to provide the community an organization that helps support persons with injury And disability or children with special needs through developmental education, meaningful Activities, extensive monitored procedures, adapted equipments and materials, and the therapeutic use of every day activities within a compassionate and collaborative environmen

Service Offered

Occupational Therapy. At Thera-able Occupational Therapy Centre, we help individuals of younger ages to achieve greater independence and improve their quality of life. Our experienced therapists work with clients to overcome physical, cognitive, and emotional challenges, enabling them to engage in daily activities with confidence.

We offer personalized treatment plans that include exercises, adaptive techniques, and assistive devices to enhance self-care, work, school, and leisure skills. Our holistic, client-centered approach empowers individuals to reach their fullest potential and lead fulfilling lives.

Shadow Class. Our Shadow Class at Thera-able Occupational Therapy Centre offers therapy sessions for children. This program provides valuable insights into therapeutic techniques, helping families understand how to support their child’s progress and development. It’s a hands-on learning experience designed to empower families to actively participate in their child’s therapy journey

Speech Therapy. At Thera-able Occupational Therapy Centre, we provide specialized speech therapy services exclusively for children. Our experienced speech therapists work with young clients to address speech, language, communication, and swallowing difficulties. Through personalized therapy plans, we help children improve their ability to express themselves, understand language, and interact with others, fostering better communication skills for everyday life

Developmental Class. Our Developmental Class at Thera-able Occupational Therapy Centre is designed for children who need extra support in developing essential skills. Through structured activities and guided learning, we focus on improving areas such as motor skills, social interactions, language development, and cognitive abilities. This program helps children build confidence, reach developmental milestones, and thrive in their everyday environments.
